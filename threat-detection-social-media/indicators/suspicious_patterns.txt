# Suspicious Social Media Behavioral Patterns

## Account Indicators
- Account created recently (< 30 days) posting urgent content
- No profile picture or generic stock photo
- Username contains random numbers (user_28473847)
- Follower/Following ratio heavily skewed
- No original posts — only shares/retweets of threat content
- Posting same link across multiple groups/communities

## Post Indicators
- Multiple exclamation marks and ALL CAPS
- Shortened URLs with no context
- DM for details with vague offer
- Requests for personal information in public thread
- Claims of official affiliation without verification badge
- Emotional manipulation language (fear, urgency, greed)

## Timing Indicators
- Posting during high-engagement hours (8-10 AM, 6-9 PM)
- Bursts of identical content within minutes (bot behavior)
- Post deletions after link clicks recorded

## Network Indicators
- Tagged in posts by other suspicious accounts
- Part of coordinated inauthentic behavior group
- Replies to trending topics with off-topic links
