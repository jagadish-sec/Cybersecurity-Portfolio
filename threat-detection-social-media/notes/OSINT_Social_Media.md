# OSINT on Social Media

## What is OSINT?

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) is the collection and analysis of information from publicly available sources to produce actionable intelligence.

## Social Media OSINT Techniques

### Profile Analysis
- Search username across platforms: `Sherlock`, `Maigret`
- Check account creation date and activity history
- Analyze followers/following for bot networks
- Reverse image search profile photo (Google Images, TinEye)

### Content Analysis
- Archive posts before deletion: `archive.today`, `Wayback Machine`
- Identify geolocation from image metadata (EXIF)
- Track hashtag usage patterns

### Network Mapping
- Map relationships between accounts
- Identify coordinated behavior (same posting time, identical text)
- Build link graphs of connected suspicious accounts

## Legal & Ethical Boundaries

- Only collect **publicly available** data
- Do not scrape in violation of platform ToS
- Do not deanonymize private individuals without consent
- Comply with GDPR / local privacy laws
